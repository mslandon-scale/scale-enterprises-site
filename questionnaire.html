Pre-Call Questionnaire

Before Your Call,
We Need to Hear From You.

You've booked your Commitment Call. That's a good sign. But we don't get on calls unprepared, and neither should you. Answer these questions honestly. No fluff. No corporate answers. The more real you are here, the more valuable your call will be.

Question 1 of 7 Paint the picture. Revenue, lifestyle, team, freedom. What does the end state actually look like for you? Be specific.
Question 2 of 7 The thing that, if it disappeared tomorrow, would change everything. Be honest.
Question 3 of 7 Something triggered you to book this call instead of kicking the can down the road again. What was it?
Question 4 of 7 Same revenue, same hours, same stress. Where does that road end? Don't sugarcoat it.
Question 5 of 7 We only take a handful of clients at a time. What makes you different? What makes you coachable and ready to execute?
Question 6 of 7 Courses, coaches, consultants, hiring, partnerships. Whatever it was. What happened and what did you learn?
Question 7 of 7 Time, money, mindset, team, spouse. Whatever it is, put it on the table now. We'd rather know before the call than during it.

Your answers are confidential and shared only with the team preparing for your call.
